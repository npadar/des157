Somewhere Over the Rainbow

Some of my travel destinations told through my keychains! Upcoming trips include New York and Las Vegas in spring 2017. Stay tuned for updates!

images

Hawaii: Summer 2013

I went to Hawaii the summer of 2013, right after graduating from high school. My family and I went island hopping for the first time around Maui, Big Island, and Oahu.

⊗

Iceland: Thanksgiving 2014

Iceland is by far my favorite place I visited. It was such a unique and fun experience watching the northern lights. We also went swimming in the Blue Lagoon, an outdoor hot spring. I could feel the snow falling while swimming.

⊗

The Netherlands: Summer 2006

I remember going to this place where the Netherlands, Germany, and Belgium shared a border. I was in three countries at once!

⊗

Italy: Summer 2012

My favorite European country is definitely Italy. I remember visiting the Leaning Tower of Pisa, the Colosseum, Venice and Florence. I ate lots of pizza, pasta, and gelato! I'm currently taking Italian right now so hopefully I will be able to use it the next time I visit.

⊗

Disneyland: Winter 2016

My most recent trip was to Disneyland over winter break. It was my first time being there with their Christmas theme which was really beautiful. It started raining really hard the first night so we had to leave early but other than that it was a very fun trip!
