Topic: Animal Conservation and The Extinction Crisis

Around 10,000 species become extinct every day. I want to make an interactive map of the world to showcase the Earth's dying species. When the user hovers over certain places, an image or video of the endangered animals will appear along with a "how to help" speal. I think I can combine my other ideas of sustainable shopping and eating into this as they are all interrelated. For example, elephants, orangatauns, and zebras are all endangered in the Indonesian jungle due to the destruction of their habitats for palm oil plantations. Consumers can help by not buying any products that contain palm oil or only buying products from companies that have pledged sustainability. It will then show a list of approved companies.
